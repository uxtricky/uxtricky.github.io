Reserve

2015 | www.reserve.com

Problem

Reserve is a digital concierge service focused on making every part of the dining experience better, for both restaurants and diners. The company would like to update their current online presence through a website redesign to properly reflect their product, value proposition and improve usability.

Solution

Reserve currently has an excellent app that provides the customer a personal dining concierge experience. For potential new customers, we would like to translate that experience onto their website through storytelling by highlighting the Reserve brand, the features of the product, and the overall dining experience.

Role

Working in a team of three, my main contributions were:

  • User Interviews & User Testing
  • Heuristics Analysis
  • Wireframes
  • Prototyping with Axure & Hype
