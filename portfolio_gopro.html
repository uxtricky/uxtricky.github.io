Go Pro

2015 | Google Carboard X GoPro

Problem

One billion people in the world have a disability that limits their function and capabilities. Due to these limitations, people with disabilities are unable to experience the thrills and joys of outdoor adventure excursions.

Solution

With the partnership of GoPro and Google Cardboard, we aim to create a mobile app that provides alternative experiences using Virtual Reality (VR) videos.

Role

Working in a team of three, my main contributions were:

  • User Interviews
  • Task Flows
  • High Fidelity Wireframes
  • Prototyping with Invision
